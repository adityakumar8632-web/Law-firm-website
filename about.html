About the Firm

Nearly three decades of trusted legal counsel — built on expertise, integrity, and an unwavering focus on client outcomes.

26+ Years of Practice
Our Story

Built Over Decades
of Trusted Counsel

X Law Firm was established in 1998 by founding partner Arjun Mehta with a singular vision: to build a law firm that combined the rigour and reputation of a large practice with the responsiveness and personal accountability of a boutique.

Over nearly three decades, we have grown from a two-partner corporate advisory practice into a full-service firm with offices across three cities, advising some of India's largest businesses, financial institutions, and government-affiliated entities.

Our growth has been deliberate. We have never expanded for its own sake. Every attorney we hire and every practice we develop is driven by one question: does this make us meaningfully better for our clients?

History

Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped X Law Firm into the practice it is today.

1998

Founded in New Delhi

Arjun Mehta establishes X Law Firm as a two-partner corporate advisory practice, with an initial focus on cross-border transactions and M&A.

2004

Litigation Practice Launched

Priya Sharma joins as Partner, establishing the firm's litigation and dispute resolution capability — expanding the firm's offering significantly.

2009

Mumbai Office Opens

Driven by the needs of financial services clients, the firm establishes its Mumbai office, gaining proximity to India's capital markets hub.

2014

Regulatory Practice Established

Vikram Nair joins, bringing deep regulatory expertise and establishing the firm's compliance and regulatory advisory practice.

2018

Bengaluru Office & Tech Practice

The firm opens in Bengaluru to serve India's fast-growing technology sector, advising startups, unicorns, and multinationals on complex deals and compliance.

2024

Recognised Among India's Top Firms

X Law Firm is ranked among India's leading corporate and litigation practices by major legal directories for the fourth consecutive year.

What Drives Us
