X Law Firm brings together attorneys with deep specialisation across four core practice areas. Our cross-functional approach means that complex matters spanning multiple disciplines are handled cohesively — with one team, one strategy, and one point of accountability.

Whether you are navigating a high-stakes acquisition, a regulatory investigation, or a multi-forum commercial dispute, our attorneys provide counsel that is grounded, strategic, and results-oriented.

01 Practice Area

Corporate Law

We advise boards, management teams, and investors on the full range of corporate matters — from incorporation and governance to complex cross-border transactions and restructurings.

Our corporate practice combines technical legal expertise with a deep understanding of business dynamics, enabling us to structure transactions and governance frameworks that are legally sound and commercially practical.

  • Mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures
  • Corporate governance and board advisory
  • Joint ventures and strategic alliances
  • Cross-border transactions and restructuring
  • Due diligence and transaction management
  • Private equity and venture capital matters

02 Practice Area

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

When disputes arise, our litigation team provides skilled, strategic representation across courts, tribunals, and arbitration forums. We focus on efficient resolution without compromising on outcomes.

We represent clients in complex commercial litigation, arbitration proceedings, and regulatory enforcement actions with a consistent emphasis on risk management and cost-effectiveness.

  • Commercial litigation and arbitration
  • International dispute resolution
  • Enforcement of arbitral awards
  • Injunctions and urgent reliefs
  • Regulatory enforcement proceedings
  • Pre-litigation advisory and risk assessment

03 Practice Area

Regulatory & Compliance

Navigating the regulatory environment requires a firm with deep institutional knowledge and practical experience across sectors. Our regulatory practice helps clients manage compliance obligations proactively and respond to regulatory scrutiny effectively.

We work closely with sector regulators and have established relationships across financial services, infrastructure, healthcare, and technology regulatory bodies.

  • Regulatory strategy and licensing
  • Compliance programme design and audit
  • Sector-specific regulatory advice (SEBI, RBI, IRDAI)
  • Government investigations and enforcement
  • Policy advocacy and regulatory submissions
  • Anti-corruption and anti-money laundering

04 Practice Area

Commercial Transactions

Our commercial transactions practice covers the full lifecycle of business agreements — from initial term sheets to complex multi-party arrangements. We draft and negotiate with precision, anticipating issues before they arise.

We act for buyers, sellers, lenders, and borrowers across a broad range of industries and transaction types, bringing commercial acumen to every engagement.

  • Commercial contracts and master agreements
  • Project finance and infrastructure agreements
  • Technology, IP, and licensing transactions
  • Real estate and property transactions
  • Supply chain and outsourcing agreements
  • Employment and labour agreements
